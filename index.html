Introduction & Purpose

This website explains screen time basics, its effects on sleep, research findings, awareness tips, surveys, and community resources for healthy digital habits.

Objectives

  • To understand the impact of excessive screen time on sleep quality.
  • To create community awareness about digital wellness and healthy sleep habits.
  • To analyze screen usage patterns among students and individuals.
  • To educate people about the harmful effects of blue light exposure.
  • To promote responsible and balanced use of digital devices.
  • To encourage better sleep routines for improved health and productivity.

Scope of Awareness

  • Creating awareness about limiting screen usage, especially during night hours.
  • Educating students and community members on the importance of quality sleep.
  • Promoting healthy digital practices such as using night mode and reducing blue light exposure.
  • Encouraging screen-free time before bedtime to improve sleep patterns.
  • Spreading knowledge about the long-term health effects of digital overuse.
  • Motivating individuals to adopt a balanced lifestyle with mindful technology usage.
