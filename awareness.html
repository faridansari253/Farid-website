Do’s & Don’ts – 10 Awareness Questions

  • Do you limit your screen time before sleeping?
  • Do you use night mode while using devices at night?
  • Do you follow the 20-20-20 rule to protect your eyes?
  • Do you avoid checking your phone immediately after waking up?
  • Do you take regular breaks while studying or working online?
  • Avoid using devices during meals.
  • Reduce brightness in dark environments.
  • Do not ignore signs of digital eye strain.
  • Avoid scrolling social media for long hours.
  • Maintain proper posture while using gadgets.

Step-by-Step Guide for Healthy Digital Use

  1. Set a daily screen time limit.
  2. Adjust brightness and enable night mode.
  3. Follow the 20-20-20 rule every 20 minutes.
  4. Keep devices away 1 hour before sleep.
  5. Maintain proper sitting posture.
  6. Schedule offline activities daily.

Digital Safety Practices

  • ✔ Use blue light filters at night.
  • ✔ Keep your screen at eye level.
  • ✔ Avoid using phone in complete darkness.
  • ✔ Protect your mental health by limiting social media.
  • ✔ Take at least 7–8 hours of sleep daily.

Real-Life Stories / Case Studies

Case Study 1: A student using mobile 6+ hours daily experienced headaches and poor sleep. After reducing screen time and using night mode, sleep improved within 2 weeks.
Case Study 2: An office worker suffered from eye strain and neck pain. After applying the 20-20-20 rule and correcting posture, symptoms reduced significantly.
Case Study 3: A teenager addicted to social media reduced usage by setting app timers, leading to better focus and improved academic performance.
