📄 Research Summary

This study examines the impact of excessive digital screen usage on sleep quality and mental well-being among students. The findings reveal that prolonged screen exposure before bedtime disrupts sleep patterns due to blue light exposure and increased mental stimulation. Awareness initiatives and digital detox practices were found to significantly improve healthy sleep habits.

🔑 Key Insights

  • Late-night screen usage is a major cause of poor sleep quality
  • Blue light exposure reduces melatonin production
  • Digital detox improves sleep and mental health
  • Community awareness programs positively change habits
